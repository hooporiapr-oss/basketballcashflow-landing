FAQ

Are sponsorships tax-deductible?

Sponsorships are recognized as marketing partnerships with Hoopotia Institute Inc. (a 508(c) nonprofit) and may be deductible as business expenses. They are not charitable donations.

Who operates the platform?

The Basketball Cash Flow platform is designed and operated by Borico Labs LLC in mission partnership with Hoopotia Institute Inc.

Do programs lose access if there’s no current sponsor?

No. We grant and maintain program access at our discretion to ensure equity and impact. Sponsorships help us expand and sustain that mission.
