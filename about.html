About Basketball Cash Flow

Basketball Cash Flow is a project created by Borico Labs LLC in partnership with Hoopotia Institute Inc., a 508(c) nonprofit organization, to give youth basketball programs, leagues, and teams a simple, powerful way to manage their finances.

Our platform helps organizers and coaches easily track income, expenses, tournaments, sponsorships, and program growth — all in one secure and accessible place. No spreadsheets, no confusion — just clarity and progress.

Our Mission

We believe that financial organization is key to sustainability in youth sports. Fiat Pa’lante — “Let there be progress” — is more than a slogan; it’s our commitment to building stronger, smarter, and more transparent programs across Puerto Rico and beyond.

Who We Serve

  • Local and regional youth basketball leagues
  • Independent travel and tournament teams
  • Nonprofits, clubs, and community programs
  • Administrators and coaches managing multiple divisions
